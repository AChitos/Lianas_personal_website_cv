Liana Drakopoulou

Organizational Psychology • HR & People Operations

Long before I could name it organizational psychology, I was drawn to the subtle patterns of human experience—why a friend's face would brighten at certain words, or how movement could release unspoken tension. As I began working in teams, that same curiosity turned toward the workplace—what sparks motivation, how culture affects well-being, and how matching talent to the right role can elevate an entire organization. I've put this interest into practice through recruitment coordination, onboarding support, and employee-well-being projects, strengthened by my psychology research and mental-health volunteer work. I'm excited to bring this people-centric perspective to innovative teams, helping them stay engaged, grow rapidly, and deliver outstanding customer value.
